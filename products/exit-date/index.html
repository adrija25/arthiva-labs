Beyond the resignation email

A career transition is two professional moments, not one.

Close the old role properly

Your final weeks can affect references, former-manager relationships and whether colleagues remember your departure as dependable or disruptive.

Research the new environment

A company website and job description rarely tell you everything. Reporting structure, communication norms, business context and role expectations deserve deliberate research.

Enter without overperforming blindly

Starting strong does not mean proving everything in your first week. Observe, ask useful questions, understand expectations and build professional trust deliberately.
