Why is CTC different from in-hand salary?

CTC means Cost to Company. It can include money and benefits that do not arrive in your bank account as monthly salary.

Variable pay, employer contributions, provident fund and estimated taxes can all create a gap between the salary number in an offer and the amount you actually receive.

CTC Reality exists to make that gap easier to understand.
