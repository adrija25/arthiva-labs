Why is this a price range?

Every collaboration differs. Audience quality, campaign goals, exclusivity, production effort, licensing and negotiation all affect final pricing.

Does follower count matter most?

No. Engagement, trust, niche authority, conversions and content quality often matter more than raw audience size.
