FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an exact sponsorship quote?

No. This calculator provides an estimated pricing range based on the information you provide. Final sponsorship fees depend on campaign scope, deliverables, licensing, exclusivity and negotiation.

Can small creators use this calculator?

Yes. Whether you have a few thousand followers or a large audience, the calculator adjusts recommendations according to your profile.

Does engagement matter more than followers?

Both matter. Strong engagement often increases brand value, especially when combined with a focused niche and high-quality content.

What is included in the premium report?

The premium report includes a detailed pricing breakdown, sponsorship recommendations, commercial licensing guidance, negotiation suggestions and a downloadable creator report.
