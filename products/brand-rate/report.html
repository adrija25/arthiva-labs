Negotiation Strategy

Practical advice for protecting your value while closing more sponsorship deals.

Opening Quote

Start negotiations using your recommended quote instead of your minimum acceptable rate. This gives you room to negotiate while protecting your commercial value.

Discount Strategy

Rather than lowering your price, offer an additional Story, Carousel or short-form asset to increase perceived value without reducing revenue.

Licensing Reminder

Paid ads, whitelisting, exclusivity and long-term commercial licensing should always be quoted separately from your standard creator fee.

Professional Tip

Confirm deliverables, revision limits, payment schedule, campaign deadlines and usage rights before accepting any collaboration.
